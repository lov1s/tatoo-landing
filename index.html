About me

So, it’s me. A girl with a great passion for tattoos and making your stories come true. My tattoo story began in Ukraine. Thanks to the trust of dozens of clients, a hobby became a matter that filled my life. Specialising in an elegant fine line style of tattooing, I uniquely design each piece to represent an individuals’ beauty and meaning behind every project.

  • Each project is unique and the price of the project can be discussed only individually, as it depends on the idea, size and placement of the tattoo. The design is included into the price of the tattoo.

  • After the tattooing process is done, I cover the result with a healing transparent film that needs to be worn for 5 days without removal. You can take a shower and wet the film. After removing the film, wash the tattoo with soap and water and moisturize it with any moisturizing cream 3-4 times a day for 2 weeks. In the first 2 weeks after the session, it is not recommended to visit a sauna, solarium or pool.

  • Tattoos become lighter over time. This is completely normal. After all it’s not paper but skin. If your tattoo loses entire lines, I’m happy to schedule a repair appointment. Please send me photos of your healed tattoo after 4 weeks of your initial appointment by email: vlr.tattooing@gmail.com
